Organic Coffee

The Gods Cafe sources their locally-roasted coffee from Campos Coffee. Our aim is delivering quality coffee and service. Campos coffee select farms that are the leaders in Speciality Coffee world for their regions, and we visit each of these farms and communities to ensure ethical farming is practised and sustainable infrastructure is in place.
